;;; This is the story of creating a word cloud:
The first step is to understand the power of a word cloud as a graphical tool.  Simple in appearance yet powerful
in performance, the word cloud allows the user to view data as an image and rapidly process insights.
Perhaps you have seen them used at a conference?  Organisers will often ask the audience to submit their
reflections of proceedings or the answer to a hypothetical question and combine all responses.
Once the data has been collected, the use of a word cloud as a display allows the audience to see all
answers, but importantly to understand which were the most and least common answers.
